Why Software Development?

The Fact

My story is different. I took the first two terms of CST in-person at the British Columbia Institute of Technology Burnaby campus from the instruction of Bruce Link and Dennis Richards, and realized I wanted to learn the material while beginning my career. I voluntarily chose to enter Flex after speeding up the process of developing personal projects. I completed Term 1 with an 88% average grade and 3.4 GPA. Term 1 was a strong introduction to programming material, and term 2 contined that trend, but this is where I decided to take a step back and spend more time developing projects and begin a job hunt.

The Solution

I ended up on a phone call with CST program head Kevin Cudihee, I told him who I am, what my plan is, and how I want to get there. This phone call single-handedly changed my path, and mistakeningly turned into a 40 minute conversation. He responded effectively and assured me my education plan would be met.


The conversation set a course for a new beginning, one that I should had started two terms earlier. It was apparent to me education was not about a race to the finish line, but a steady, consistent, journey for knowledge, that must be developed and harnassed into a foundation that lasts a lifetime. That foundation began the day I started my programming journey.
